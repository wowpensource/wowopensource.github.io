About

I'am Sandeep

Sandeep boasts over a decade of distinguished experience in mid to senior-level management within the upscale dining scene of New York City. His expertise lies primarily in Michelin 1- and 2-star establishments, a testament to his exceptional skills in the industry.

As a certified sommelier, Sandeep stands out as one of the very few, if not the first, hailing from his home country. He's undertaken advanced sommelier training and successfully navigated the rigorous examination process through the esteemed Court of Master Sommeliers Association America. This achievement is especially notable considering the stringent prerequisites and the limited number of individuals worldwide who hold the esteemed title of advanced sommelier.

Book

PRE-SHIFT: Secrets to Restaurant Service

"Pre-Shift: Secrets to Restaurant Service" unlocks the world of fine dining like never before. From creating inviting atmospheres to perfecting service, this book offers expert insights and real-life examples for those in the hospitality industry or curious about behind-the-scenes of fine dining.

This exceptional guide dives deep into service nuances, sales tactics, and guest issue resolution, presenting challenging scenarios and insightful conversational examples. Whether you're a beginner, manager, owner, or aspiring restaurateur, the book equips with the skills needed to create unforgettable dining experiences. It's a must-read for delivering exceptional guest service in any industry.
